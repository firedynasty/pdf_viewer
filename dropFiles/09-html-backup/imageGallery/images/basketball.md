To **“square to the basket”** means positioning your body so that your shoulders, hips, and feet are aligned and facing directly toward the basketball hoop. This is crucial for maintaining balance, control, and accuracy when shooting. Squaring up helps ensure that your shooting form is consistent, allowing for better precision and making it easier to repeat successful shots.



**Key Points of Squaring to the Basket:**



​	1.	**Feet Positioning:** Both feet should be shoulder-width apart, and your toes should point toward the basket. Some players prefer turning their feet slightly to the side for comfort.

​	2.	**Hips and Shoulders:** Your hips and shoulders should also face the basket, allowing your shooting arm to extend directly toward the hoop without any awkward body twists.

​	3.	**Balance and Control:** This position provides the necessary balance to help control the force and direction of your shot, improving overall shooting mechanics.
